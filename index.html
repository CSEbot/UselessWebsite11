Our History

Bethesda Game Studios is an award-winning development team known for creating some of the industry's most successful and beloved games. Founded in 2001, the studio has grown to over 400 employees and has received numerous accolades, including over 200 Game of the Year awards. Our latest game, Starfield, is set to release in 2023 and promises to take players to new and uncharted worlds.
